Expi:

Expi is a bot I created for my friend Newmentor for his discord server, it comes with several commands that connect his players more towards the game. I had a lot of fun creating this discord bot and have been developing on it since 23/10/2017 (October the 23rd), this bot has been my first project using discord.js and node.js. It was not an easy and straight forward road, there were many hilarious bumps and annoyances with it. Many said that I should of studied Javascript more in able to create better code and less mistakes but, to me, creating a project allows more learning and you are more likely to remember the language better!
Extra Links that this project is involved in: RobloxManjaro, Official Roblox Kilimanjaro Blog

Box:

This is my first ever twitter bot, I am very proud of it so far and have already gained a couple people that are interested in what it does. I do not expect this project to grow into something more major as most of my projects featured on here are just my little side projects. This bot posts RNG messages (consisted of a sentence: "hello everyone today I am going to pet my cat") every hour, it also posts breaking news from NASA whenever they upload something, it retweets and follows (or stalks depending on what you think of this bot haha) certain twitter accounts which should help spread its existence, if you @mention the bot you will be greeted with an image of someone facepalming, every day it gives followers a nice little message to cheer them up, it greets everyone that is a new follower (the little group it has is called the box army, follow if you must), it has a couple other features too!
